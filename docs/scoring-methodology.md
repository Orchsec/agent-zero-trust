# Scoring Methodology

The score starts from weighted categories totaling 100 points. Failed rules subtract category
points by severity: Critical 18, High 10, Medium 6, Low 3. Category scores never drop below zero.

Readiness levels:

- 90-100: Strong
- 75-89: Good
- 60-74: Needs improvement
- 40-59: High risk
- 0-39: Critical risk
